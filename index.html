Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 2, 2026

MetricViz Research ("we", "our", or "us") is an independent research initiative focused on health data visualization. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle data received via the Garmin Connect Developer Program.

1. Data Minimization Principle

We adhere to a strict policy of data minimization. We only request access to the specific biometric data points required to render visualizations. We do not request access to GPS location data, user contacts, or social graph data.

2. Data Collection & Scope

Upon your explicit authorization via OAuth verification, the application accesses the following telemetry from the Garmin Connect API:

  • Biometrics: Heart Rate Variability (HRV), Resting Heart Rate, Stress Scores, and Sleep Architecture (Rem/Deep/Light stages).
  • Activity: Daily step counts and intensity minutes.

3. Data Storage & Architecture (Client-Side Only)

To ensure maximum privacy and security, this application operates on a client-side architecture.

  • Volatile Storage: Data retrieved from the Garmin API is processed within your web browser's volatile memory (RAM) for the purpose of generating immediate visualizations.
  • No Remote Database: We do not transmit, store, or archive your personal health data on external servers or databases.
  • Session Data: Once you close the browser tab or refresh the page, the fetched health data is cleared from memory.

4. Third-Party Infrastructure

This application is hosted on GitHub Pages. While MetricViz does not collect logs, GitHub Inc. may collect technical access logs (such as IP addresses) for security and reliability purposes, in accordance with the GitHub Privacy Statement.

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, trade, or analyze your personal data for advertising purposes. No data is shared with third parties.

6. Your Rights (GDPR & CCPA)

Under applicable data protection laws, you have the right to revoke access to your data. You may disconnect this application at any time via your Garmin Connect Account Settings. Since we do not store your data on our servers, no further action is required to request deletion of historical data.
