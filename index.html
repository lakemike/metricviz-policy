Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 3rd Jan 2026

MetricViz ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle data received from the Garmin Connect Developer Program.

1. Data Collection

We collect health and activity data specifically authorized by you via the Garmin Connect API. This may include, but is not limited to: Heart rate data, sleep metrics, activity logs, and stress scores.

2. Use of Data

The data collected is used strictly for the purpose of:

  • Generating scientific visualizations and dashboards.
  • Conducting feasibility analysis on health data trends.
  • Technical testing of the application's data ingestion capabilities.

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data to third parties. Data is not shared with any external entities (including advertising networks or data brokers) unless explicitly required by law.

4. Data Storage and Security

Data retrieved from the Garmin API is stored locally on the user's device for the duration of the visualization session. We employ industry-standard security measures to protect data integrity.

5. User Rights

You may revoke this application's access to your Garmin data at any time via your Garmin Connect Account Settings. Upon revocation, any stored data associated with your account will be permanently deleted from our records.

6. Contact Us

If you have questions about this policy, please contact us at: metricviz AT gmail.com
